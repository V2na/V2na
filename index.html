SPLIT/SECOND: REBORN






In 2010, Disney Interactive Studios released the arcade racing game Split/Second: Velocity.
Not long after its release, the developer, Black Rock Studio, went bankrupt, and the game never received a sequel.

Split/Second: Reborn is a recreation of the original game, built in Unreal Engine 5.
